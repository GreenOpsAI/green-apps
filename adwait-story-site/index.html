The Twin Revolutions: AI & The Energy Transition

A Conversation with Adwait

My Journey & Thesis

1. My Foundation

I am a mechanical engineer and a self-taught AI specialist with 10+ years of experience implementing AI for various companies. In 2023, I made a deliberate pivot to apply these skills to a more purposeful domain: sustainability.

2. My Pivot

I recently graduated with an MBA in Sustainability, driven by the conviction that the energy transition is fundamentally a business problem. Corporations hold the greatest leverage and responsibility to lead this change, and I am focused on driving that impact from within.

3. Post-Pivot Experience

As a Research and Strategy Intern at DNV Energy, I learned the dynamics of U.S. competitive energy markets. Now, as the Data and AI Lead at Elephant Energy, I am operationalizing the installation of heat pumps and other residential electrification technologies.

4. What I’m Realizing

  • The world is being reshaped by two forces at once: AI and the energy transition.
  • AI is scaling fast, creating new possibilities for productivity—but also driving massive energy demand.
  • The energy transition is urgent: moving toward electrification, distributed grids, and renewable supply.
  • These two forces are deeply linked, and I am committed to building my career at their intersection.

5. My Positioning

My focus is at the intersection of AI and the energy transition. As we enable large-scale, generalized AI, there's a parallel need for specialized, distributed AI. Both require intelligent energy use powered by renewables. My goal is to build deep expertise in how new businesses and innovations can leverage AI in a fundamentally sustainable way.

"The future depends on how we apply intelligence and how we power it sustainably."
